Hello! My name is Natalie Chow and I'm a User Experience and Visual designer.

I'm currently a senior at the University of Washington (Seattle) double majoring in Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE), and Interdisciplinary Visual Arts (IVA). A mouthful, I know. HCDE is centered around learning user research, user experience design and interaction design, while IVA is focused on the fine arts like drawing, printmaking, and painting. I am graduating in June 2019.

In relation to design, some of my interests and passions include;

  • How technology can help those with physical and mental disabilities
  • How technology can support people's mental health and mental wellbeing
  • How design can alleviate certain stigmas, like women in gaming and dialogue about periods

A bit more about me now! I grew up in the pacific northwest, just outside of Seattle. I love all sorts of tea, and I'll only drink coffee if it's around 30% coffee (70% milk and sugar and other flavoring). I also really enjoy interior design, fashion, and artwork. You can check out my pinterest to get a sense of my style.

In my free time you can find me playing video games, painting/drawing, listening to music, or hanging out with friends.

If you have any question, comments, conundrums, or quibbles, feel free to email me at natalie.michelle.chow@gmail.com.
